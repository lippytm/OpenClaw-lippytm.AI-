# Project Role

## Purpose
`OpenClaw-lippytm.AI-` is the OpenClaw research and experimental learning hub for the wider lippytm ecosystem.

## Primary Objectives
- explore OpenClaw-style architectures and workflows
- document experimental agent patterns and reference designs
- support AI learning through sandboxed experimentation
- compare internal MyClaw operations with open research approaches
- create a safe place for prototyping and architecture notes

## Inputs
- research questions
- experimental workflows
- architecture ideas
- upstream references
- agent and automation concepts

## Outputs
- research notes
- experimental documentation
- comparison docs
- sandbox learning artifacts
- prototype architecture guidance

## Connected Repositories
- `MyClaw.lippytm.AI-`
- `lippytm-lippytm.ai-tower-control-ai`
- `Web3AI`

## KPIs
- clarity of experiments
- usefulness for AI learning
- safety and separation from production systems
- quality of architectural documentation
